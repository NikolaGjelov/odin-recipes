Perfect Polenta

Description

Perfect polenta is easier to make than you think! This top-rated polenta recipe will rival what you'd get at your favorite Italian restaurant.

Ingredients:

  • Four cups of water for every cup of polenta
  • Salt
  • Polenta
  • Three tablespoons of butter
  • Cheese

Steps:

  1. Add the polenta to salted boiling water.
  2. Simmer until the polenta is slightly thick.
  3. Cover and continue cooking until the polenta is very thick.
  4. Stir in the butter and cheese.
  5. Serve and enjoy!
